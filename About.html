FILIP LINHART

Filip Linhart was born in the former Czechoslovakia. After the completion of his business studies in 2015, he moved to Hong Kong to be closer to his biggest passion - traveling in Asia. He first visited the continent in 2013 when he won his free flight ticket to represent the Czech republic in the international case study competition in  Thailand. He and his girlfriend took their backpacks and have been traveling since then off the beaten tracks in countries like Vietnam, Thailand, the Philippines, Taiwan or China. He is fascinated by the contrasts between the fast-developing cities and less developed, often frozen in times, countrysides, natural wonders and local people's lives. He learning his photography skills the go by experimenting with lights, composition and the overall atmosphere. His photos has been featured in travel magazines in the Czech Republic as well as won several prices in the student photography contests.

You can follow Filip on Instagram, Facebook or check their personal blog Skrosnou.cz
